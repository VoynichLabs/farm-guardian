# 06 — hawk_scream

Aerial predator. **Must be a real species native to Hampton, CT** — red-tailed hawk, Cooper's hawk, or sharp-shinned hawk. The plan's H1 effect is contingent on the chickens' innate-or-experiential recognition of *the right* raptor; a generic "raptor cry" recording from an unrelated species muddies that test.

Target: 3 exemplars, peak-normalized to -3 dBFS, clamped to 3-6 s. Public-domain or CC0. Macaulay Library has many but most are not redistributable — prefer USFWS or Wikimedia Commons. Document species in the MANIFEST `notes` column.

**Welfare note:** highest-arousal category. Plan caps to 1 per cohort per day.
